FORMSUBMIT INTEGRATION GUIDE

Your contact form is now connected to FormSubmit (https://formsubmit.co/), a free email form submission service that doesn't require registration, API keys, or backend code.

HOW IT WORKS:
------------
1. When a user submits the form, it sends the data directly to FormSubmit's servers
2. FormSubmit forwards the submission to your email address (lallen6@sheffield.ac.uk)
3. The user sees a success message on your website

CURRENT CONFIGURATION:
--------------------
- Recipient Email: lallen6@sheffield.ac.uk
- Subject Line: "New submission from Kausalyze website!"
- Form Format: Table layout
- CAPTCHA: Disabled
- Success Redirect: Back to your contact page with a success parameter
- Honeypot Protection: Enabled (anti-spam measure)

CUSTOMIZATION OPTIONS:
--------------------
You can modify these settings by changing the hidden input fields in Contact.jsx:

1. Change recipient email:
   - Update the action attribute in the form tag:
   - action="https://formsubmit.co/your-new-email@example.com"

2. Change subject line:
   - <input type="hidden" name="_subject" value="Your custom subject line" />

3. Enable CAPTCHA:
   - <input type="hidden" name="_captcha" value="true" />

4. Change success redirect:
   - <input type="hidden" name="_next" value="https://your-website.com/thanks" />

5. Change email template:
   - <input type="hidden" name="_template" value="box" /> (options: table, box, plain)

ADDITIONAL FEATURES:
------------------
FormSubmit offers other features you can add:

1. File attachments:
   - Add enctype="multipart/form-data" to your form tag
   - Add <input type="file" name="attachment" /> to your form

2. CC/BCC recipients:
   - <input type="hidden" name="_cc" value="cc-email@example.com" />
   - <input type="hidden" name="_bcc" value="bcc-email@example.com" />

3. Auto-response:
   - <input type="hidden" name="_autoresponse" value="Thank you for your submission!" />

TROUBLESHOOTING:
--------------
- First-time use: You may receive a confirmation email from FormSubmit to verify your email address
- Spam filters: Check your spam folder if you don't receive submissions
- Rate limits: FormSubmit has usage limits (50 submissions per day on the free plan)

For more information, visit: https://formsubmit.co/
